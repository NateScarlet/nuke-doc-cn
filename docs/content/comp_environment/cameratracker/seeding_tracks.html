Seeding Tracks

Before you start tracking your footage, you can select specific points in your sequence which you know youâ€™ll want tracked. This is called seeding and you can do it as follows:

1.   Right click on the point in your footage you want to seed.
2.   Select Tracks > Seed track.

An orange cross indicates the location of your track, and when you click Track Features, your seeded point is tracked with the other, automatically selected features.
