Solving Your Camera

To solve your camera:

1.   After you’ve created your locators, you can select which locators are to be used in the solve by toggling the Active box next to each locator on the Locators tab.
2.   On the ProjectionSolver tab, click Solve Camera. ProjectionSolver solves your camera and gives you a solve error value in the Solve Error field. Should any of the frames in your footage fail to solve, you’ll see a message box with tips that might help. You can also try adjusting the Solver and Lens controls for better results.
