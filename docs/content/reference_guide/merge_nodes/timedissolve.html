TimeDissolve

Dissolves between two inputs, starting the dissolve at the in frame and ending at the out frame. You can specify the dissolve curve over time.

Sets the frame to end the dissolve.

N/A

Use the curve to define how the dissolve behaves in the frame range:

• 0 - the frame before the in frame.

• 1 - the frame after the out frame.
