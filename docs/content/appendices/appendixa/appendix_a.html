Appendix A

Nuke Studio Hotkeys

This appendix lists the hotkeys used by both applications, along with their function. In general, contiguous areas of the keyboard are assigned to related functionality. The left-hand keys on a QWERTY keyboard are used for tool selection and actions, the right-hand side for playhead manipulation including source/record and insert/overlay, the arrow keys for selection and nudging, and the numeric pad for timecode entry.
