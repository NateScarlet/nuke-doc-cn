Scopes Preferences

These preferences set the Black and White points for Histogram and Waveform Out of Range warnings individually, in the range 0-1.

Preference

Default Value

Description

Black point

 0.00

Sets the black out of range warning level.

White point

 1.00

Sets the white out of range warning level.

Luma/chroma encoding

 REC 709

Sets how the scopes display luma and chroma information by selecting the rec601 or rec709 standard.
