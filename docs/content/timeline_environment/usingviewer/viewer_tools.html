Timeline Viewer Tools

The Viewer has two sets of tools for manipulating your media: the Viewer tools and the playback tools. The Viewer tools, located at the top of the Timeline Viewer, are used to affect the mouse pointer as you move over the Viewer, and to select Viewer preferences:
